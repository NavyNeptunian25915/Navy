Pieces Movement

There are eight types of pieces in the board, (Captain, Battleship, Tank, Submarine, Jet, Helicopter, Soldier).

Captain Movement: Knight.

Battleship Movement: Queen.

Tank (Capture but Movement): (Rook but Knight).

Submarine (Capture but Movement): (Bishop but Knight).

Jet (Capture but Movement): (Knight but Rook).

Helicopter (Capture but Movement): (Knight but Bishop).

Soldier (Capture but Movement): (1-2 squares forward straight but 1-2 squares forward diagonal).

There is promotion logic, they can promote into any piece but a captain, but if it lands on the “H” column then it will automatically promote only into a captain, that is ONLY when the old captain is captured, otherwise it cannot move in the last row IN the “H” column.
